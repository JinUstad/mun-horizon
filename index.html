Founder's Message

"Welcome to Horizon MUN Debate, where young minds converge to shape the future of diplomacy and international relations."

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to develop critical thinking, public speaking, and negotiation skills while gaining deep insights into global affairs. Through rigorous debate and diplomatic simulation, we prepare the next generation of leaders.

Join us in this transformative journey where every voice matters, every perspective counts, and every delegate contributes to building a better world through dialogue and understanding.

- Founder, Mohd Umar

Committees & Agenda

Explore our diverse range of committees addressing critical global issues

UNGA

United Nations General Assembly

Addressing global challenges including climate change, sustainable development, and international cooperation. Delegates will debate resolutions on pressing worldwide issues.

  • Climate Action & Environmental Policy
  • Global Peace & Security
  • Human Rights & Social Justice

RAJYA SABHA

The Upper House of the Indian Parliament

Simulating the Indian parliamentary system, focusing on domestic policy, governance, and legislative processes that shape the nation's future.

  • Economic Policy & Development
  • Education & Healthcare Reform
  • Digital India & Technology

AIPPM

All India Political Parties Meet

A unique platform bringing together representatives from various political parties to discuss national issues and find common ground for India's progress.

  • National Security & Defense
  • Electoral Reforms
  • Federal Structure & Governance

HCC

Historical Crisis Committee

Experience pivotal moments in history through dynamic crisis simulation, making decisions that shaped our world and learning from historical perspectives.

  • World War II Allied Conference
  • Cold War Crisis Management
  • Independence Movement Leaders
